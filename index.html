Description:

I built this website to showcase my skills in their current state, I tried to involve many aspects people seek in a responsive website. Aspects like, media queries, modals, animations, intersection observers, parallax backgrounds and a nice color theme to please the eyes.

This website will never truly be done, I will continue to update it as I develop my developer skills, I hope this website can give you a clear example of what i'm currently capable of. Hopefully, it also tilts you're mind in hiring me for whatever job I applied for at the time :)
